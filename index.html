Computer Science and Engineering

B.Tech

The study of computing is the study of the process of abstraction and its power, the practice of design and method of best implementation and mathematical theory of computation and its limits. The list of knowledge areas at the undergraduate level thus include Discrete Structures, Algorithms and Complexity, Computational Theory, Computer Architecture, Programming Language Theory and Implementation, Database Systems, Operating Systems, Computing and Software Engineering. Emphasis is also given to hardware architectural design and software system design.

Department of Computer Science and Engineering and Information Technology has state of the art labs, machines with high configuration and latest software required for teaching and research. The department endeavors to impart quality education to students in the areas of computer science and engineering and information technology. The computer industry is one of the fastest growing segments of our country and that growth promises to continue in future as well. An engineering degree in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) equips you with strong practical and theoretical knowledge of computer software and hardware.

Our Services

Client/Server Computing Lab

The Client/Server Computing Lab provides extensive computing resources for first year students to perform their practicals . This includes high-performance UNIX/Linux based computers. The laboratory provide services as a commuter center common to all first year students

Networking Lab

The students gain knowledge of networking, switched, network simulation and computer communications. Static Routing, Dynamic Routing Protocols, LAN switching, Transport Layer Protocols: UDP and TCP, NAT, DHCP, DNS, and SNMP are the main focus areas. This Lab consists of computer systems with 2 GB RAM and Routers.

Operating System Lab

Linux kernel development, Distributed systems, Android applications development are the key areas of the laboratory. Students also get trained on Linux system administration and widows systems administration. Student learn the internal working and administration of various operating systems.

Software Engineering Lab

Software engineering lab focus on industry standard software development practices. Students use full SDLC Practices in learning industry standards project development. IBM Rational Software Architect is main development tool available here. The main focus area on Agile development
